What Is Bathtub Reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing, also known as bathtub refinishing, is a cost-effective way to restore the surface of your bathtub. This process involves cleaning, repairing, and applying a new coating to give your bathtub a glossy, like-new appearance. Whether you have chips, stains, or just general wear and tear, reglazing can bring your tub back to life.
